<-[terms]
Operation

Pops the first term (Operator or Operand), quoted. More...

Input
{A B}
Output
{A}{B}
Examples
  • <-[terms] {A B}

    {A}{B}

  • <-[terms] {{A} B}

    {{A}}{B}

  • <-[terms] {A B }

    {A}{B}

  • <-[terms] {A {B} C}

    {A}{{B}
    C}

  • <-[terms] {}

    {}{}
